Why MediCollab?

We are solving the biggest problems in healthcare record management by giving you full ownership and transparency.

Patient-Centric Control

You approve every record. Nothing gets added to your permanent health timeline without your consent, ensuring accuracy and transparency.

Works Everywhere

Your digital health timeline is portable. It works across different hospitals, cities, and even countries, ending data fragmentation for good.

Emergency Ready

With your permission, doctors can instantly access your verified medical history in an emergency, enabling faster and safer treatment.

For Patients

Take control of your health journey. Build a secure, lifelong digital record of every prescription, report, and bill. Share your history with new doctors in a single click and never worry about losing a paper record again.

  • Build your permanent Health Timeline.
  • Approve or reject hospital uploads.
  • Get smart reminders for appointments.

For Doctors & Hospitals

Collaborate with your patients transparently. Reduce paperwork, build trust, and gain instant access to a new patient's verified medical history (with their consent). Our platform simplifies record-keeping and improves quality of care.

  • Upload records directly to patient profiles.
  • Receive patient approval for data accuracy.
  • Access complete patient history with one click.
